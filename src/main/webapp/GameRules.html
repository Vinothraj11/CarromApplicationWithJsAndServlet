Game Rules

Press 1 to pocket your coin

Press 2 to pocket opponent coin

Press 3 to pocket queen coin

Press 4 to give striker to opponent

Press 5 to pocket the striker


Score

Get 1 point while pocketing your coin

Lose 1 point while pocketing opponent coin

Lose 1 point while pocketing striker

Get 3 point while pocketing queen coin
